USAGE:
======

1. DISPLAY DEVICE:
   - Open DisplayDevice/DisplayDevice.ino in Arduino IDE
   - Install LovyanGFX library
   - Upload to ESP32-2432S022

2. SENDER DEVICE:
   - Choose an example (01_Basic recommended for testing)
   - OR copy DisplayClient.h to your own project
   - Wire: TX → Display GPIO 3, GND → GND
   - Upload and enjoy!
